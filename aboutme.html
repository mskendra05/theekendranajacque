About Me

I am a Haitian-American, born and raised in Hampton, Virginia, and a proud Georgia resident since 2015. Growing up as the middle child of 7 in a "nomad" household, I learned early on that adaptability is a superpower. Moving frequently between Virginia, Florida, and Georgia shaped my ability to find my footing anywhere.

Despite the changes in scenery, my commitment to excellence remained anchored. I live by the philosophy that trying is the goal and giving up is not an option. This drive for "anything but ordinary" is what I carry into my technical career in Information Technology.
